How We Work

The Artisan's Journey

Every masterpiece begins with a vision and ends with a legacy. Discover the meticulous steps we take to bring light to life.

01

Precision Cut

Each piece is hand-cut from premium artisan glass to follow the intricate patterns of the design.

02

Leading & Soldering

Pieces are carefully assembled using traditional lead cames or copper foil, then soldered for strength.

03

Refinement

The final piece is cemented, polished, and inspected to ensure it meets our heritage quality standards.

Our Materials

The Glass Library

The soul of any stained glass piece resides in the glass itself. We maintain an extensive archive of hand-rolled, mouth-blown, and custom-textured glasses. From ripple glass that mimics flowing water to shimmering opalescents, our materials are selected to shape and filter daylight beautifully.

  • Hand-selected Wissmach & Kokomo sheets
